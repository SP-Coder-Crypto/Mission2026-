The Mission Community Youth Center Inc. is a non-profit Christian based operation whose goal is to partner with the community for a brighter future. Our focus is on providing an environment for families of all cultural backgrounds, to participate in the building blocks for a better future. M.C.Y.C. will aid in empowering our children, families, and the community to reach its maximum potential. We believe in inclusion and cultural diversity. We are committed to providing a safe learning environment while focusing on highlighting quality care throughout all the developmental stages. We pride ourselves in being the only community center in the area to offer parents a piece of mind. By offering dependable childcare services for all 3 shifts and weekends, we can afford them the opportunity to accept second and third shift employment, knowing that their children are in good hands with qualified, caring staff members that delivers consistent and dependable high-quality care.
